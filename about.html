Our Story

Leading RMG Manufacturer Since 2005

Kaizer Knitwears Ltd. has been at the forefront of sustainable and ethical garment manufacturing for over 18 years.

Starting as a small facility with just 100 machines, we have grown into a state-of-the-art manufacturing hub employing over 2,500 skilled workers and producing 3 million pieces monthly.
